Chennai

The Flight

Major domestic carriers operate direct flights to Chennai and from the city, while all major international carriers fly from and to the city too.

The City

Chennai formerly known as Madras, is the capital city of Tamil Nadu, the southernmost state of India. It is the state's primate city both in area and population and is located on the Coromandel Coast of the Bay of Bengal.

The History

The Pallavas of Kanchi built the areas of Mahabalipuram and Pallavaram during the reign of Mahendravarman I. They also defeated several kingdoms including the Cheras, Cholas and Pandyas who ruled over the area before their arrival.

The Food

Chennai, India has a vibrant street food culture and is known for its rich and diverse culture. The food culture of Chennai is mainly South Indian and includes dishes like Dosas, Idlis, vada, etc.
